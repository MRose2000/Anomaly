What is this game about?

OMR (Our Metal Robot) has been abandoned on the Anomaly - a ship - and must destroy all of the corrupted robots that are on the Anomaly to find it's way home.

How did we make this game and website?

We made this game with HTML5, CSS3, Unity 5.5, Construct, and Photoshop.

Who made this game and website?

Nick made the game, Ellenor made the graphics, and Madeline made the website.

Where did we make this game and website and how long did it take?

We made this game at the Governors Institute of Vermont , Information Technology and Digital Media. It took approximately 42 hours.
