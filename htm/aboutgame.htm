What is the object of the game?

You play as OMR (Our Metal Robot) who has been abandoned on the Anomaly – an ancient flying vessel from an ancient civilization. OMR's objective is to destroy all the corrupted robots on the ship in order to escape.

What are the controls?

To move around, you use the keyboard. To move forward you press W. To move left you press A, to move right, press D, and to move backwards, press S. J is left click, and K right click. You shoot at the keys to collect them.
